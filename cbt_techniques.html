Top CBT Techniques for Mental Health

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is one of the most effective methods for improving mental health.

1. Thought Reframing

Replace negative thoughts with balanced ones. Example: “I can’t do this” → “This is challenging, but I can learn.”

2. Grounding Technique

Use the 5-4-3-2-1 method: - 5 things you see - 4 things you feel - 3 things you hear - 2 things you smell - 1 thing you taste

3. Behavioral Activation

Take small actions even when you don’t feel like it. Action reduces overthinking.

4. Journaling

Write your thoughts daily to gain clarity and reduce stress.

Conclusion

CBT techniques are simple but powerful when practiced daily.
