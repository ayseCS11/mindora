Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in?

Open the Mindora AI app and use your registered email and password to sign in. You can also use the "Continue with Google" option.

How can I reset my password?

Tap "Forgot password?" on the Sign In screen and follow the instructions sent to your email. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see it.

How do I use the mental wellness exercises?

After signing in, start a session in the app and Mindora will guide you through exercises based on your mood and recent activity.

Is Mindora a therapist?

No, Mindora is a supportive AI companion. It provides guidance based on mental wellness principles but does not replace professional therapy.
